Complete Rules

🎯 Objective

Create as many words as possible using letters from the hive. Score points to advance through 10 challenging levels!

📏 Word Rules

  • Words must be at least 4 letters long
  • Words must include the center (yellow) letter
  • Letters can be used multiple times
  • Only letters in the hive can be used

⭐ Scoring

  • 4-letter words: 1 point
  • 5+ letter words: word length points
  • Pangrams (all 7 letters): Bonus points!

⏱️ Time & Target

You have 5 minutes per level. Score 20 points to unlock the next level!

⌨️ Controls

  • Click letters or use your keyboard
  • Press Enter to submit words
  • Press Backspace to delete
  • Press Space to shuffle letters
